World Capitals

Ten cities. Endless reasons to go.

01

Tokyo

Japan · East Asia

Ancient shrines, neon streets, and more Michelin stars than anywhere on earth. Tokyo is endlessly, gloriously alive.

Senso-ji Shibuya Akihabara
02

Paris

France · Western Europe

The City of Light still earns every word written about it. The Eiffel Tower at dusk is worth every cliché.

Eiffel Tower The Louvre Montmartre
03

Cairo

Egypt · North Africa

Stand before the last Wonder of the Ancient World and feel the full weight of human history. Absolutely unmissable.

Pyramids The Nile Khan el-Khalili
04

Buenos Aires

Argentina · South America

Midnight steakhouses, cobblestone tango, jacaranda-lined boulevards. Buenos Aires lives life at full volume.

La Boca Tango Recoleta
05

Nairobi

Kenya · East Africa

Lions at sunrise, world-class coffee at noon. The only city on earth with a national park on its doorstep.

National Park Giraffe Centre Westlands
06

Bangkok

Thailand · SE Asia

Golden temples, jaw-dropping street food, and an energy that makes every other city feel half-awake.

Grand Palace Wat Pho Street Food
07

Mexico City

Mexico · N. America

Built on the ruins of an Aztec empire. The murals are magnificent; the tacos are life-changing. Come hungry.

Templo Mayor Frida Kahlo Tacos
08

Reykjavik

Iceland · N. Europe

Northern Lights, volcanic geysers, and midnight sun. The world's northernmost capital is extraordinary.

Northern Lights Blue Lagoon Golden Circle
09

Wellington

New Zealand · Oceania

The world's coolest small capital: brilliant museum, legendary coffee, and the home of Middle-earth filmmaking.

Te Papa Weta Workshop Harbour
10

Athens

Greece · Mediterranean

The Acropolis has watched over this city for 2,500 years. Democracy, philosophy, and drama were born here — and the souvlaki is excellent.

Acropolis Parthenon Monastiraki Plaka
