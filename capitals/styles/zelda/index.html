The World Capitals

— ✦ — ✦ —

Ten sacred cities, each holding a piece of the Great Journey.

♥♥♥ ♥♥♥ ♥♥♥ ♥
I

Tokyo

Japan · Eastern Realm · Pop. 13.96M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

You have discovered the Sacred City of the East, where ancient shrines glow beside towers of light. The Keeper of Michelin guards this realm. Cherry blossoms fall like stars each spring — seek this place and you shall be forever changed.

Key Sites
Senso-ji Temple Shibuya Crossing Tsukiji Market Meiji Shrine
II

Paris

France · Western Realm · Pop. 2.16M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

The City of Eternal Light. An iron tower rises above all, a beacon that has guided travellers for over a century. Deep within the Louvre dungeon lie the world's greatest treasures — gather your courage before you enter.

Key Sites
Eiffel Tower The Louvre Montmartre Seine River
III

Cairo

Egypt · Desert Realm · Pop. 21.3M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

The Realm of Ancient Power. Three great pyramids rise from the sands — their builders wielded magic no longer understood. The Nile flows as it has for 5,000 years. Stand before the last Wonder and feel the Triforce pulse in your hands.

Key Sites
Pyramids of Giza Egyptian Museum Khan el-Khalili The Nile
IV

Buenos Aires

Argentina · Southern Realm · Pop. 3.07M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

A passionate kingdom where the dance of tango unlocks hidden pathways through the city. The midnight feasts restore full health. Jacaranda trees bloom purple each November, marking the secret season of power.

Key Sites
La Boca Recoleta Cemetery San Telmo Market Teatro Colón
V

Nairobi

Kenya · Wild Realm · Pop. 4.4M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

The Wild Realm, where lions walk at dawn against a skyline of towers. Giraffes are the guardians of the eastern gate. The sacred brew of single-origin coffee restores full magic power. No other realm offers such wonders side by side.

Key Sites
Nairobi National Park Giraffe Centre Karen Blixen Museum Westlands
VI

Bangkok

Thailand · Temple Realm · Pop. 10.5M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

The Golden Temple Realm, home to hundreds of sacred shrines. The Grand Palace holds the Emerald Buddha, one of the realm's most powerful artefacts. Follow the Chao Phraya river to discover hidden temples on both banks.

Key Sites
Grand Palace Wat Pho Chatuchak Market Chao Phraya
VII

Mexico City

Mexico · Volcano Realm · Pop. 9.2M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

Realm built upon an ancient kingdom's ruins, in a valley ringed by sacred volcanoes. Two civilisations' power flows through every stone. The Great Taco has legendary healing properties known to all who make the pilgrimage.

Key Sites
Templo Mayor Frida Kahlo Museum Zócalo Xochimilco
VIII

Reykjavik

Iceland · Frost Realm · Pop. 128K

Dungeon Cleared ✓

The Edge of the World Realm. At night, the Aurora Borealis illuminates the sky with divine magic. Sacred hot springs glow in winter darkness. The Hallgrímskirkja spire is a beacon seen from all surrounding realms.

Key Sites
Northern Lights Hallgrímskirkja Blue Lagoon Golden Circle
IX

Wellington

New Zealand · Harbour Realm · Pop. 215K

Dungeon Cleared ✓

A small realm of great heart, perched on a harbour at the world's edge. The Weta Workshop holds the secrets of illusion magic. Te Papa houses the realm's greatest treasures. Strong winds test the resolve of all travellers — persevere.

Key Sites
Te Papa Museum Weta Workshop Mount Victoria Cuba Street
X

Athens

Greece · Ancient Realm · Pop. 3.15M

Dungeon Cleared ✓

The Eldest Realm — birthplace of democracy, philosophy, and the arts of war. The Acropolis is the final temple, standing for 2,500 years in defiance of all darkness. Climb the hill, reach the Parthenon, and the Great Journey is complete.

Key Sites
The Acropolis Parthenon National Museum Monastiraki Plaka District
