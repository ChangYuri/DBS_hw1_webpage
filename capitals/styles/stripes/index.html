World Capitals

Ten extraordinary cities.

01

Tokyo

Japan · East Asia · 13.96M

Ancient shrines meet neon skyscrapers in the world's most electrifying city. More Michelin stars than anywhere on earth. Tokyo is endlessly generous to anyone curious enough to explore it.

Senso-ji Temple Shibuya Crossing Meiji Shrine Akihabara
02

Paris

France · Western Europe · 2.16M

The City of Light still earns every superlative. The Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, the candlelit bistros — Paris is a city that has been written about forever and still surprises you every time.

Eiffel Tower The Louvre Montmartre Le Marais
03

Cairo

Egypt · North Africa · 21.3M

The Pyramids of Giza are still the most staggering thing humans have ever built. Cairo wraps 5,000 years of history around a roaring, magnificent modern metropolis.

Pyramids of Giza Egyptian Museum Khan el-Khalili The Nile
04

Buenos Aires

Argentina · South America · 3.07M

Midnight dinners, cobblestone tango, and jaw-dropping steak. Buenos Aires lives at full volume and invites you to do exactly the same.

La Boca Recoleta Cemetery San Telmo Teatro Colón
05

Nairobi

Kenya · East Africa · 4.4M

The only capital on earth with lions on its doorstep. At dawn, giraffes cross the savanna as the city skyline rises behind them. Africa's most dynamic city is nothing short of extraordinary.

Nairobi National Park Giraffe Centre Karen Blixen Museum Westlands
06

Bangkok

Thailand · Southeast Asia · 10.5M

Golden temples, legendary street food, and an energy that makes every other city feel half-awake. Bangkok will overwhelm your senses — and you'll love every second of it.

Grand Palace Wat Pho Chatuchak Market Chao Phraya
07

Mexico City

Mexico · North America · 9.2M

Built atop an Aztec empire, ringed by volcanoes. The murals are magnificent, the history is staggering, and the tacos will permanently raise your expectations.

Templo Mayor Frida Kahlo Museum Zócalo Xochimilco
08

Reykjavik

Iceland · Northern Europe · 128K

Northern Lights dance overhead. Geysers erupt across the plains. Hot springs warm you through the winter. The world's northernmost capital is staggeringly, joyfully beautiful.

Northern Lights Hallgrímskirkja Blue Lagoon Golden Circle
09

Wellington

New Zealand · Oceania · 215K

Tiny but mighty. World-class museum, obsessive coffee culture, and the Weta Workshop where Middle-earth was brought to life. Wellington is the world's best small capital, by a wide margin.

Te Papa Museum Weta Workshop Mount Victoria Cuba Street
10

Athens

Greece · Mediterranean · 3.15M

The Acropolis has watched over this city for 2,500 years. Democracy was born here, philosophy was shaped here, and the souvlaki is still the best you'll ever eat.

The Acropolis Parthenon National Museum Monastiraki
