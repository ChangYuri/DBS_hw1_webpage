THE PEOPLE DEMAND TO KNOW THEIR WORLD

01

Tokyo

East Asia · Japan · 13.96M Strong

The workers of Tokyo built the greatest city on earth. Fourteen million citizens. Zero excuses. The trains run on time. The temples stand. The ramen burns with collective purpose. This is what ambition looks like. Study it.

Senso-jiShibuyaTsukijiMeiji
02

Paris

W. Europe · France · 2.16M Strong

The Eiffel Tower was built in 26 months. What have you built lately? Paris shows the world what is possible when a people refuse to accept mediocrity. The Louvre holds more ambition than most nations have ever produced.

Eiffel TowerThe LouvreMontmartreSeine
03

Cairo

N. Africa · Egypt · 21.3M Strong

Four thousand five hundred years. The Pyramids still stand. Twenty-one million people who know what endurance means. They built without machines. They built without permission. They built forever. You will learn here or you will leave.

PyramidsMuseumKhan el-KhaliliThe Nile
04

Buenos Aires

S. America · Argentina · 3.07M Strong

The tango was born in the streets, not in the academies. Passion is not taught — it is demanded. Buenos Aires does not wait for you to be ready. Midnight is not late here. Arrive prepared or do not arrive at all.

La BocaTangoRecoletaTeatro Colón
05

Nairobi

E. Africa · Kenya · 4.4M Strong

The lions do not apologise for being lions. Nairobi does not apologise for being Nairobi. Four million people building the future at sunrise with the wild on the horizon. This is the only city on earth with a national park inside it.

National ParkGiraffesKaren BlixenWestlands
06

Bangkok

SE Asia · Thailand · 10.5M Strong

Four hundred temples. Every one covered in gold that was earned, not stolen. Bangkok does not whisper. The street food alone would defeat most capitals. Ten million people who understand what beauty costs and pay it without complaint.

Grand PalaceWat PhoChatuchakChao Phraya
07

Mexico City

N. America · Mexico · 9.2M Strong

They built this city on a lake and then built it again on the ruins of an empire. Nine million people who know that nothing is impossible. The Zócalo is the second largest public square on earth. Size is a statement. Receive it.

Templo MayorFrida KahloZócaloXochimilco
08

Reykjavik

N. Europe · Iceland · 128K Strong

Born from volcanic fire. Illuminated by the Northern Lights. Built by people who looked at impossible terrain and said: this will do. This is what happens when a people refuse the comfortable and choose the extraordinary instead.

AuroraBlue LagoonHallgrímskirkjaGolden Circle
09

Wellington

Oceania · New Zealand · 215K Strong

Small in size. Enormous in will. Wellington produces more per capita than cities ten times its size. The museum. The coffee. The film studio that created Middle-Earth. Do not underestimate this city. It does not forgive that mistake.

Te PapaWetaMt VictoriaCuba St
10

Athens

Mediterranean · Greece · 3.15M Strong

The original. Democracy was invented here. Philosophy was invented here. Theatre was invented here. Mathematics was invented here. Every idea you have ever had began in Athens. You are merely continuing their work. Know your origins.

The AcropolisParthenonNational MuseumMonastiraki
